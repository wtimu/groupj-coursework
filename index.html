About us

Who we are

Group J SACCO is a Savings and Credit Cooperative Organisation established by members of Group J at Victoria University, Kampala. We exist to provide a safe, transparent, and accessible platform where members can save regularly and access affordable credit.

Licensed by the Bank of Uganda, we operate on the principles of democratic control, full member participation, and total financial transparency. Every member can see the cooperative's performance, track their savings, and contribute to its growth.
