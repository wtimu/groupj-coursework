Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a member?

Visit our Registration page, fill in your full name, National ID, phone number, email, and create a password. Once you accept the terms and conditions and submit, your account will be reviewed and activated within one business day.

How do I make a savings deposit?

You can deposit via Mobile Money (MTN or Airtel) or bank transfer. Use your Member ID as the reference number when making a payment. Deposits are usually confirmed within a few hours and will appear in your dashboard.

How do I apply for a loan?

Log in to your member dashboard, navigate to Loan Services, and complete the online loan application form. You will need to have an active savings history. Loan decisions are communicated within 2 to 3 business days.

What is the interest rate on loans?

Group J SACCO offers loans at a flat interest rate of 2% per month. The exact repayment schedule will be provided to you at the time of loan approval and will also be visible in your member dashboard.

How do I recover my password?

On the Login page, click on "Forgot password?" and enter your registered email address and National ID. A password reset link will be sent to your email within a few minutes.

How long does a withdrawal take?

Withdrawal requests are processed within 1 to 2 business days. You will receive a notification once your withdrawal has been approved and sent to your mobile money or bank account.

Can I access my account from a phone?

Yes. The Group J SACCO platform is accessible from any device with a web browser, including mobile phones, tablets, and computers. No app installation is required.

Who do I contact if I have a problem with my account?

You can use the contact form above to send us a message, or call us directly on +256 700 123 456 during office hours. Our support team will respond within one business day.
